Hi, I'm BHARGAV DEVMURARI — CINEMATOGRAPHER who Crafting Emotions in Motion.

hi, I’m Bhargav here, a professional Wedding Cinematographer based in Surat, Gujarat. With over 6 years of experience in wedding cinematography and 5 years in film editing, I’ve dedicated my creative journey to capturing timeless emotions and stories. I graduated from Veer Narmad South Gujarat University, and before pursuing filmmaking full-time, I also spent one year preparing for UPSC, which taught me focus and discipline. Cinematography isn’t just my profession — it’s my passion. I’m always eager to learn new techniques, explore fresh ideas, and push creative boundaries in every project I take on. Over the years, I’ve collaborated with several renowned studios like Timeless Memory, Ivory Films, Studio Cloud9, Firefly Weddings, Maruti Photo Art, Vision Stories, VR Photo Studio, and Golden Photography. I’ve been trained by Canon influencer Abhishek Kanani (CinematicAb, Ivory Films) and Suryash Raghuvanshi, under whom I also worked as an assistant cinematographer for one year — gaining hands-on experience in visual storytelling and production. I’m always driven by passion, growth, and the desire to tell stories that touch hearts and visuals that live forever..
